Introduction about School:

My school name is Mission Girls High School, the oldest traditional educational institution of the century, and is situated in the western part of Rajshahi city, adjacent to the court in the area surrounded by many trees. Environment The Church of Bangladesh is situated on 122 khata or 2.013 acres of land on the mission campus of the Rajshahi Deanery. On the south side of this school, the premises of Shaheed Minar, the office of the Superintendent of Police, the office of the Deputy Commissioner, and the court, are located on the west side. Also, Nawabganj Road passes through the south side of this school.The reputed school was established in 1888 by missionaries of the Presbyterian Church as a primary school to promote female education in the region. In 1967, the school was upgraded to Junior High School. In 1969, it was converted to middle school, and in 1985, it was included in MPO. Since its inception, the school has been spreading the light of education in society. In 1976, a student named Kawsar Jahan from this school participated in the SSC examination in the science department and secured the first position in the Rajshahi Education Board. Moreover, the students who passed from this school are well established in different levels of society, including doctors and engineers.The current president of the managing committee of this century-old holy school run by the Church of Bangladesh, Right Rev. Ice Cream:

Ice cream has always been my favorite dish and a delightful treat on a hot summer day. The smooth texture, the variety of flavors, and the cool sensation it provides make it an irresistible temptation for me. One of the reasons why ice cream is my favorite dish is because of its mouthwatering texture. The creaminess and the velvety feel of the dessert as it melts in my mouth are truly incredible. It is like a soft and smooth pillow of deliciousness that instantly brings a smile to my face. The texture of ice cream allows me to savor every spoonful and experience a moment of pure pleasure. Another aspect of ice cream that contributes to my love for it is the infinite possibilities of flavors to choose from. Whether it is classic flavors like chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry, or exotic combinations like salted caramel, cookie dough, or mint chocolate chip, there is always a flavor to suit any craving. I enjoy exploring new flavors and trying out different combinations, as it adds an element of surprise and excitement to the whole ice cream experience. The wide range of flavors allows me to customize my dish and indulge in my personal preferences. Not only does ice cream satisfy my taste buds, but it also provides me with a refreshing sensation, especially during the sweltering summer months. The coldness of the dessert soothes my palate and provides a temporary respite from the heat outside. It is like a mini-vacation for my senses, as I close my eyes and relish in the frosty delight that ice cream brings. Whether enjoyed on a cone, in a cup, or as part of a sundae, the coolness of ice cream is the perfect antidote to beat the summer heat. Furthermore, ice cream holds a special place in my heart as it is often associated with cherished memories and joyful moments. From childhood trips to the ice cream parlor with my family to celebrating birthdays with a big bowl of ice cream, these experiences evoke warm feelings of happiness and laughter. Ice cream has a way of bringing people together, creating a sense of unity and joy. It is a dish that is often shared amongst loved ones, enhancing the bond and creating beautiful memories. In conclusion, ice cream is undeniably my favorite dish due to its delightful texture, the vast array of flavors, the refreshing sensation it provides, and the memories it evokes. It is a treat that never fails to bring a smile to my face and adds a touch of sweetness to life's moments. Whether it is a simple scoop or a decadent sundae, ice cream is a never-ending pleasure that I will continue to savor with great delight.
